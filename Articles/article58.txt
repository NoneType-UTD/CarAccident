Gardaí are investigating the cause of a fire at an abandoned warehouse in Dublin.
The blaze started at around 2pm yesterday afternoon at the premises on Richmond Road in Drumcondra.
Up to five units of the Dublin Fire Brigade attended the scene, and it took 16 hours to put the fire out.
The warehouse has been sealed off by gardaí.
Units from Dublin Fire Brigade were also called out to deal with bonfires near railway lines at Baldoyle and Kilbarrack.
